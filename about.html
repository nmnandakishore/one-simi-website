About Us
Who we are

One Simi is an organisation that works to address critical cultural, racial and global issues by providing meaningful and sustainable volunteer services to international communities across Simi Valley, California under the leadership of the mayor.

Our Vision
What we want to be

One Simi subscribes to the belief that differences in race, gender, nationality, sexual orientation, physical ability, and background bring richness to our community and, as such desires to serve as a forum that brings about cultural awareness, sensitivity and respect to the holistic needs of all of members of the community.

Our Goals
What we want to do

Treat every community member with respect and dignity; challenge initiatives that are not in line with One Simi’s Mission; Encourage growth through a welcoming environment. Serve as a model to our youth in the pursuit of our community objectives

Our Values
What defines us

Honesty, Integrity and Professionalism Family centric, friendly environment Hard work, participation and achievement Cross-cultural public dialogue and civility Openness and the ability to speak freely
